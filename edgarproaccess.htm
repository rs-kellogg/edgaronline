ACCESSING EDGAR Pro

The School's licensing agreement with EDGAR Pro allows access for up to 10 concurrent users to their web application at pro.edgar-online.com, for Kellogg faculty, students and staff. The button below will log you in their site. If the login fails, please try later.

Please remember to log out once you are done. Click on "Logout" on the top right corner of the EDGAR Pro screens. Access to the site is monitored -- people not affiliated with Kellogg should not be given access to this page. Do not change the password to the account or change any preferences on the account.
